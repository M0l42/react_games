Game of Life

This is a zero player game who try to simulate life.

It's not a big project and not too complicated game, but i always liked the Game of Life and always wanted to code one someday.

And because I didn't have prior experience in React I thought it would be a good experience and a good training.

Here's the tutorial i used on Youtube

Snake

I think we all know what game Snake is, but this time, he's playing on his own. How? With path finding algorithm !

For the moment you have the choice between Dijstra and A* algorithm but more are to come !

I tested my algorithm on an other project in Python using pyglet. Here's the repo

There's some improvement in the way

  • For the moment, when he's trying to finding the apple, he doesn't know his body is moving along.

    There's sometimes he can find the apple but have to play with the environment to make the part of him which are blocking him disappear.

    So I want him to be aware of his moving body

  • The shortest path is not always the best one. Sometimes it's better to find another way so you're not stuck after finding your current goal
